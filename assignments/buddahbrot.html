Buddah Brot January 2004 Jared Tarbell Based on code by Paul Bourke Technique invented by Melinda Green

Buddah Brot is unlike the two previous works I have showcased. The feeling is less organic. This is because Jared's inspiration for this piece is a modification of the Mandelbrot Set (a set of complex numbers) by Melinda Green. Despite this, Buddah Brot still invokes awe and wonder in its viewers. The title, referencing the founder of Buddhism, and the divine glow of the white particles gives this piece a spiritual aura.
