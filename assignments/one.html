Artist: Jared Tarbell

Jared Tarbel is a computer science graduate of New Mexico State University and a cofounder of the popular online marketplace Etsy. However, before Jared's entrepreneurial venture, he had created (and is still creating) some of the most beautiful examples of generative art I have ever seen. He is proficient in divising deceptively simple algorithms that produce images rivaling nature, invoking feelings of awe and wonder.
