Substrate June 2003 Jared Tarbell

Substrate is another excellent piece by Jared which, despite being computationally generated, captures the essence of nature. To me, the image resembles some type of crystallization. I thoroughly enjoy how Jared utilized his Sand Stroke algorithm in tandem with this piece to heighten the organic feel it exuberates. Looking at this earlier version, it is apparent how much natural essence is lost without it.

In an interview, Jared states he was inspired to create this piece while in a Santa Fe coffee shop, observing the cracks in a sticker on the window cooked by the sun. This tendency for Jared to look at the natural world through algorithmic lenses is something I greatly admire.
